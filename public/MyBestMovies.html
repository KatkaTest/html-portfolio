The Best Movies According to Katka

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Jak vytrhnout velrybě stoličku

Czech family commedy about boy Vašek, who grow up only with his mother and never met his father as he was told his father, who was mountaineer, died on Annapurna before Vašek was born. Vašek decided to look for some new father and possible husband for his mother, and it happens that by accident he meets guy who is his real father.

Pelíšky

Czech commedy movie about Czech family life back in communism time.

Popelka

Czech version of cinderela fairytale, that is also very popular in Norway.
